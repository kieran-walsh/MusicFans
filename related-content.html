Related Content Computation

Enter a valid username, and related content posts will be found for them, based on the posts they currently have bookmarked.

How this external program works:

  1. For every content item, get a list of the people who like it
  2. For every person in this list, get a list of content they like
  3. Remove posts bookmarked or created by the given user
  4. Then return a list of related posts, and add to the database
